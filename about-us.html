About Us

We are a Christian-based Non-profit organisation that was founded in 1999 where a stainless-steel Baby Saver Box was built into the wall of a church in Johannesburg to address the rapid increase of child abandonment, establishing the beginning of the Door of Hope.

We focus on rescuing, caring for, and supporting vulnerable and abandoned babies and young children. Our work is centred around stepping in during crisis situations where infants have been left without care, ensuring they are immediately protected, fed, and given medical attention when needed. From there, we dont just provide short-term care, we actively work toward giving each child a stable, loving, and permanent family environment.

Beyond immediate rescue and childcare, we also extend our support to the biological mothers who are in distress or are unable to care for their babies, offering safe surrender options so children aren't left in dangerous situations. Our work is deeply rooted in protecting life at it's most vulnerable stage and restoring dignity into families where children can grow up with love and stability.

Our Mission

It is to empower vulnerable children and families by providing comprehensive support and creating lasting, loving homes.

Our Vision

To create a world where every child has the opportunity to thrive in a safe, loving environment regardless of their circumstances. We aim to become a trusted platform that makes a real difference in everyday life.

Our Values

  • Compassion:We are driven by empathy and a deep commitment to the well-being of every child and family we serve.
  • Integrity:We operate with honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions and decisions.
  • Collaboration:We believe in the power of partnerships and work together with communities, donors, and other organizations to achieve our mission.
  • Respect:We treat every individual with dignity and respect, valuing their unique stories and circumstances.
  • Excellence:We strive for excellence in everything we do, continuously improving our services to better serve those in need.
