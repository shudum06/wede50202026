Our Services

We are committed to providing care, protection, and support to vulnerable children. Our services are designed to give every child a safe and loving environment.

Child Rescue and Protection

We provide immediate care and protection for abandoned children and defenseless children, ensuring their safety and well-being

Temporary Safe Cares

Children are placed in a safe and nurturing environment while long-term solutions are arranged.

Long-term Care and Adoption

We assist in the adoption process by helping place children in loving and permanent homes. We work with families to ensure each child finds the care and stability they deserve.

Family Support and Reunification

Where possible, we work to reunite children with their families and provide ongoing support.

Community Outreach and Education

We engage with communities to raise awareness and provide support to families in need
